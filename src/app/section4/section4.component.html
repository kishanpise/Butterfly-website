Butterfly families.


Hedylidae

Hedylidae

Common Name: American moth-butterflies.
Charecterstics: Small, brown, like geometrid moths; antennae not clubbed; long slim abdomen.

Hesperiidae

Hesperiidae

Common Name: Skippers

Charecterstics: Small, darting flight; clubs on antennae hooked backwards.

Lycaenidae

Lycaenidae

Common Name: Blues, coppers, hairstreaks.
Charecterstics: Small, brightly coloured; often have false heads with eyespots and small tails resembling antennae.

Nymphalidae

Nymphalidae

Common Name: Brush-footed or four-footed butterflies.
Charecterstics: Usually have reduced forelegs, so appear four-legged; often brightly coloured.

Papilionidae

Papilionidae

Common Name: Swallowtails.
Charecterstics: Often have 'tails' on wings; caterpillar generates foul taste with osmeterium organ; pupa supported by silk girdle.

Pieridae

Pieridae

Common Name: Whites and allies
Charecterstics: Mostly white, yellow or orange; some serious pests of Brassica; pupa supported by silk girdle.
